Humidity

The humidity plot shows overall the northern hemisphere is wetter than the southern hemisphere, mostly because it's the wet season in the north.

You can also observe that there is a high concentration of data points at 100% humidity between -20 and 20 degrees latitude. This is where the tropics are located, and where you'll find the majority of the world's rainforests (Amazon, Congo, etc.).
